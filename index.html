Carolina Thai Healing Arts offers professional Thai massage, Cupping
massage therapy, Acupressure, Reflexology, and our signature East meets
West massage.

Experience the deep relaxation and healing that a Thai massage will bring
into your life. This ancient healing art from Thailand is performed on a
comfortable floor mat while fully clothed. Sometimes we use hand crafted
herbal compresses, traditional balms, liniments or cups to release areas of
pain and congestion.

Energy lines (Sen in Thai, similar to meridians in Chinese medicine) are
unblocked and soothed, joints are moved through their range of motions
and muscle tension is released with rhythmic compression, stretching and
rocking motions. Thai massage is truly a unique and wondrous therapeutic
art.

At Carolina Thai Healing Arts we also offer the unique East meets West
massage. We merge the flow of a Swedish massage combined with the
deep therapeutic benefits of Eastern massage
techniques. The massage
will leave you feeling relaxed, refreshed and calmly energized to move with
grace through your day.

During this massage you will be draped in fresh linen and an aromatic
blend of herbal oils will be applied directly to your skin to sooth tired
muscles and refresh your spirit. Varying degrees of pressure will be applied
to pressure points and sen (energy) lines and we may also include
stretches and range of motion to begin to remove tension and accumulated
tightness in your muscles.

This massage includes the best of both Eastern and Western massage to
leave you relaxed and rejuvenated.
